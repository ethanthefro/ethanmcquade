ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Ethan McQuade, a Director, Cinematographer, and Videographer based out of Virginia Beach, VA. I cut my teeth shooting music videos, and have since explored many diverse fields like advertisements, sports, documentaries, and live events.

I created a production company called LOWBAT, where some of my closest friends and I take on creative projects together. Video production often brings together groups of individuals that wouldn't otherwise cross paths, and I find beauty in seeing many people with their own complicated origin stories come together to create art and preserve a moment in time.

I'm a firm believer that great storytelling, thoughtful composition, and strong performances are what make videos pop. These timeless techniques are employed in every project I take on, and I always strive to deliver visual excellence, no matter how simple or grand the scale of what's being worked on.
